Our story

Run by the family that pours your coffee.

Pandaisia sits right on the sand at Komi, on Chios' southeast coast — the kind of place people mean to stop at for a coffee and end up staying for lunch, then sunset. Christos and Smaro keep the pass moving and the sunbeds free, no minimum spend required.

The dough is made in‑house every morning, which is why the pizza keeps showing up at the top of "best on the island" lists. Everything else — the frappes, the mastiha cocktails, the waffles — follows the same rule: simple, fresh, and unhurried.
